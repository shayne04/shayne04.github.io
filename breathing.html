Mindful Meditations

Simple breathing exercises to relax the mind and soothe your nervous system.

Deep and thoughtful breathing exercises help to calm the nervous system and can act as a “reset” in times of unrest. While breathing is an automatic process – meaning our body does it for us without us even having to think about it – the breath has a direct relationship with our thought’s feelings and emotions. Mindful breathing exercises help us to bring awareness to mind-body connection and are shown to help with both relaxation and energy.

Mindful Breathing Exercise

This guided breathing meditation will help you ground yourself in the present moment. By focusing your attention on your breath, you can quiet the mind and relax the body. Use this exercise any time you’re feeling anxious, overwhelmed, or simply need a pause.

Make sure you're sitting comfortably, away from distractions, and simply follow along with the guide.
